Enter your desired username in the checker tool above. It probes WhatsApp's servers (wa.me) to detect if the username is taken or available. For the most reliable result, also verify directly in the WhatsApp app under Settings → Account → Username.

What are WhatsApp username rules?

WhatsApp usernames must be 5–30 characters long, start with a letter, and can only contain lowercase letters (a-z), numbers (0-9), periods (.), and underscores (_). Consecutive periods or underscores are not allowed.
